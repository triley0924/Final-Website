Products

PlainsFishing offers a variety of soft plastic fishing baits that can be made to any possible color. There are 4 different baits offered by PlainsFishing. They are listed with a breif description below.

Curly-Tail Worm: The Curly-Tail Worm mimics many different prey the fish feed on including leeches, worms, or other insects.

Craw: The craw is simple and compact, but really does a great job of mimicking a crawfish which is a big part of the fish's diet.

4-Inch Swimbait: The 4-Inch Swimbait mimics other smaller minnows and bait fish that bigger fish tend to eat. This is a good bait for big fish!

Senko: This is a fan favorite bait. Between it's simple design, and easy for beginners to use, this bait flat out catches fish. It mimics a worm struggling in the water.
